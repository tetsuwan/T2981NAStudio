Security Vulnerability Reporting

T2981NA Studio takes the security of our applications and user data seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our products, we encourage you to report it responsibly.

How to Report

Email: tetsuwan28go@gmail.com
Subject line: [Security Vulnerability Report]

What to Include

  • A description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • The potential impact of the vulnerability
  • Any suggestions for remediation (optional)

Our Commitment

  • We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 72 hours.
  • We will investigate and work to resolve confirmed vulnerabilities promptly.
  • We will not take legal action against individuals who report vulnerabilities responsibly.

Scope

This policy applies to all applications and services published by T2981NA Studio, including games available on the Facebook Instant Games platform.
