Data Deletion Request

← Back to T2981NA Studio

T2981NA Studio respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you would like to delete your data associated with our games, you can do so using one of the following methods:

Method 1: Delete Data Within the Game

Open the game on Facebook Messenger, go to the main menu, and tap "Reset Stats" to delete all your game data (win/loss/draw records and saved progress).

Method 2: Remove the Game from Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings.
  2. Navigate to "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings" > "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "Reversi" in the list of apps.
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke the app's access to your data.

This will remove all data associated with the game from your Facebook account.

Method 3: Contact Us Directly

If you would like to request complete deletion of all your data, please contact us through the contact form on our website. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data We Store

Our games store only the following data through Facebook's player data APIs: your game statistics (wins, losses, draws) and active game sessions. We do not maintain separate databases or servers. All data is stored within Facebook's infrastructure.
