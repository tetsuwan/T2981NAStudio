T2981NA Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates games on the Facebook Instant Games platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our games.

1. Information We Collect

When you play our games through Facebook Instant Games, we may receive the following information provided by Facebook:

  • Profile information: Your display name and profile photo
  • Player ID: A unique identifier assigned by Facebook
  • Game data: Your game statistics (wins, losses, draws), scores, and game progress
  • Context data: Information about multiplayer game sessions (e.g., which friend you are playing with)

We do not collect your email address, phone number, friend list, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and operate our games
  • Save your game progress and statistics
  • Enable multiplayer features (friend matches and random matches)
  • Display leaderboards
  • Improve our games and user experience

3. Advertising

Our games may display advertisements through Facebook Audience Network. These ads are served by Facebook and are subject to Facebook's own advertising policies and data practices. We do not share your personal data with third-party advertisers. For more information about how Facebook handles advertising data, please refer to Facebook's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored through the Facebook Instant Games platform using Facebook's player data storage APIs. We do not maintain separate servers or databases for your personal information. Your data is protected by Facebook's security measures.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your game data may be visible to other players only through in-game features such as leaderboards and multiplayer game sessions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Reset your game data within the game settings
  • Remove the game from your Facebook account at any time, which will revoke our access to your information
  • Contact us to request deletion of your game data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
